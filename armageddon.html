Armageddon
Armageddon is the epitome of all robotics events of NIT Durgapur. It is a combination of manual and autonomous robotics events in which only the best may win! In the manual events, gears, motors and switches must come together to make a bot that can overcome all obstacles whereas sensors, micro-controllers and code create their magic to make a bot that must perform tasks in the autonomous event. Add in the absolute thrill and excitement of a Robo-war and, bingo! You have the perfect recipe for an entertaining event. A definite highlight of every Aarohan, Armageddon sees participation from reputed colleges from and outside Durgapur every year.
Manual events
(‘Be an Avenger’ and ‘Furia’)
Perfect synergy between bot and controller is the essence of the manual events of Armageddon. Motors, gears and switches come together to create a robot that must be expertly controlled in order to overcome the set obstacles and to lay claim to the prize. Since the teams compete among each other, winning these events is all about precision and timing. This Aarohan, come and compete in the manual events of Armageddon and show that you have it in you to be a winner!
Robowars
Can you build a strong, indestructible robot which can survive attacks from the other robots? Are you confident that your robot is stronger than that of your opponents? Well, Robowars is the event for you. Every tool and weapon is fair game as long as it is sufficient to guarantee you victory. So, bring out your cutters, flippers and mini-flamethrowers and get started on this adrenaline-pumping flagship event of Armageddon.
Autonoumous events
(Arbitrium)
A brilliant combination of mechanical design and logical coding. Solve the problem statement. Build an autonomous cum semi-autonomous bot which can navigate through paths involving lines, walls and other obstacles. Can your bot follow the correct path without deviating? It’s time to prove your engineering knowledge and skill through your own robot. The better your skill, the better your bot. Exciting prizes involving cash and other goodies are waiting to be unleashed!
