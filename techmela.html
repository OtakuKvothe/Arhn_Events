Techmela
Tech Mela, the Annual Science Exhibition of NIT DURGAPUR is organized by CCA during AAROHAN 2019. Exhibits, Prototypes and Models covering different genres of Science and Technology are invited. Students regardless of their stature are encouraged to present their innovative ideas or explain confusing theories to their fellow fraternity. Deserving presenters get full reimbursement of their project expenses and a chance to take their project to Rashtrapati bhavan and present it.
